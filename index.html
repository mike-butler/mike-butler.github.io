About

Hi I'm Mike, a web developer adept at applying front end technology to produce pixel-perfect results from UX design wireframes/mockups and create responsive mobile web applications that look and behave beautifully across devices.

I am currently working with a cool new startup in the Seattle area helping to define and build their web presence. Prior to that, I worked at C Space on their proprietary global online community platform, developing in the front and middle tiers on the Microsoft stack.

When I'm not working on a website, I have to stay active cycling, or running to keep up with three active kids. As an Italian foody, I enjoy baking bread and applying engineering precision to making pizza dough.
